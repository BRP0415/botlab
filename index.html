About BRP BotLab

BRP BotLab is a hub for Discord bot enthusiasts, developers, and server owners looking to innovate their communities. We offer a full-service ecosystem: from development support and bot customization to deployment strategies and community engagement tools.

📚 BRP BotLab FAQ

❓What is BRP BotLab?

BRP BotLab is a community dedicated to building and improving Discord bots. Whether you're looking for a custom bot, need support, or want to collaborate with developers, you've come to the right place!

🛠️How do I request a custom bot for my server?

Head to the #bot-requests channel or use the /request command. Provide detailed info like what features you want, your server's needs, and any inspiration or visuals. Our devs will take it from there!

🐞How can I get help if a bot isn’t working properly?

If you're running into issues, visit the #support channel. Describe the issue clearly (include screenshots if possible). We aim to respond within 24 hours!

🎭What are the different roles in this server?

We have a range of roles, including Bot Developers, Moderators, Executives, Premium Members—and of course, our valued Members like you!

💎How can I become a Premium Member?

Support us by boosting the server to get the Premium Member role. This unlocks perks like priority support, access to private channels, exclusive commands, and more!
